Welcome to Coffee Break Stories, an online literary journal dedicated to short prose.

Coffee Break Stories wants to give you something to read - no matter how little time you have. We publish short prose that entertains, surprises, and makes use of its form in original ways. We are always looking for stories that do something in the short space they have - stories that make us feel, think, laugh or cry. Consider submitting your own writing to Coffee Break Stories here.
