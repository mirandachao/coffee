SUBMIT TO COFFEE BREAK STORIES

Coffee Break Stories publishes short prose that entertains, surprises, and makes use of its form in original ways. We are looking for stories that do something in the short space they have - stories that make us feel, think, laugh or cry. We are open to both experimental and traditional forms of prose. Currently, Coffee Break Stories only publishes prose written in English.

SUBMISSION GUIDELINES

Please keep the following guidelines in mind when submitting to Coffee Break Stories:

  • Submit your work to the appropriate category based on its word count.
  • Submissions that exceed the word count will not be considered.
  • Submissions are read blindly by the Coffee Break Stories Editorial Board. Please make sure that your name does not appear in the text of the document you are submitting.
  • We do not accept work that has been previously published by another literary publication. While we will consider work published on a personal blog or website, please let us know if your submission appears elsewhere on the Internet.
  • We encourage simultaneous submissions but require an immediate withdrawal of your submission if your work is accepted elsewhere.
  • You may submit to more than one category, but we encourage you to wait to hear back from us before submitting a second piece in the same category.
  • We do not consider erotica or pieces with excessive violence. We reserve the right to tag your work with appropriate content warnings for readers.
  • Contributors retain their copyright. If your work is published elsewhere after being published on our site, please give us credit for publishing your work originally.
